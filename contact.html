Welcome to Connect

I'm delighted that you're interested in getting in touch. Whether you're looking to book a performance, explore collaboration opportunities, discuss repertoire, or simply share your thoughts about music, I'd love to hear from you.

As a pianist and composer passionate about both traditional and lesser-known works, I'm always excited to connect with fellow musicians, concert organizers, and music enthusiasts. I particularly welcome opportunities for chamber music collaborations and performances that explore the rich but often overlooked corners of the piano repertoire.
